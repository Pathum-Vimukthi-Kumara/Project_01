Project Work and Collaboration

Overview

Welcome to the Project Work and Collaboration phase of Boost Camp! This part of the program is designed to provide hands-on experience through real-world projects, foster teamwork, and encourage creative problem-solving. By working collaboratively, you will not only apply the skills you've learned but also gain valuable insights from your peers.

Key Activities

  1. Introduction to Group Projects
    • Objective: Understand the purpose and structure of the group projects.
    • Activity: Presentation explaining the project scope, goals, and deliverables. Overview of team roles and expectations.
  2. Brainstorming and Planning Sessions
    • Objective: Generate ideas and create a project plan.
    • Use project management tools (e.g., Trello, Asana, Microsoft Teams) to keep everyone on the same page.
  3. Project Work
    • Objective: Execute the project according to the plan.
    • Activity: Teams work on their projects, applying skills learned in workshops. Regular check-ins with mentors for guidance and support.
  4. Project Presentations
    • Objective: Present the completed projects and receive feedback.
    • Activity: Teams present their projects to peers and mentors. Feedback is provided to help improve future work.
