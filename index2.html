Evaluation and Future Planning

Overviwe

As Boost Camp concludes, it's time to evaluate your journey, reflect on your achievements, and plan your future steps. This phase is designed to provide you with constructive feedback, celebrate your progress, and help you set a course for continued growth and success.

Key Activities

  1. Self-Reflection and Assessment
  • Objective: Reflect on your experiences, challenges, and growth during Boost Camp.
  • Activity: Complete a self-assessment questionnaire focusing on your goals, achievements, and areas for improvement.
  1. Peer and Mentor Feedback
  • Objective: Gain insights from peers and mentors to understand your strengths and areas for growth.
  • Activity: Participate in feedback sessions where peers and mentors provide constructive feedback on your performance and contributions.
  1. Project Showcase
  • Objective: Present your final projects and celebrate accomplishments.
  • Activity: Teams present their projects to the entire Boost Camp cohort, followed by a Q&A session and a celebration of achievements.
  1. Future Planning Workshop
  • Objective: Develop a plan for your continued personal and professional growth.
  • Activity: Attend a workshop on goal setting and action planning. Create a detailed plan outlining your next steps, resources needed, and timelines.
